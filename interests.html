My Interests

Basketball

I am deeply interested in basketball and have been since middle school. I not only love to play the sport but I also love to watch. I have been a mavericks fan since 2017 and am now a secondary lakers fan because of the Luka trade. This has also been a reason for my love for data science, with the intersectionality between basketball and sports analytics which I also love.

I spend much of my free time going to the ymca or the park with my friends and play basketball. A big part of basketball I love is the community and the closeness of everyone who plays. I can go to the park and play pickup with people whom I've never met and instantly bond based on our shared love for the game. I think I could play for the whole day by myself if presented with the opportunity.

Baking

In recent years I have started to enjoy baking more and have picked it up as a hobby. It started with my hometown friends and I baking something every Thanksgiving as a tradition and has become something that I regularly do now for enjoyment.

My favorite things to bake are pumpkin and pecan pies, but I also love to make chocolate chip cookies and guava cake. I have also been experimenting with Pakistani desserts like Mitai but that is still a work in progress.
